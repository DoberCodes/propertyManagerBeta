Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: February 2026

Maintley is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We are continuously working to improve the user experience for everyone and to apply relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards wherever reasonably possible. These guidelines help make web content more accessible for people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments.

Ongoing Efforts

We are actively working to:

Maintain clear navigation and consistent structure

Support keyboard navigation

Provide proper labeling for forms and interactive elements

Maintain sufficient color contrast

Improve compatibility with screen readers and assistive technologies

Regularly review and test accessibility using automated and manual tools

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we recognize that some areas may still need improvement.

Feedback

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our website or application, or if you have suggestions for improvement, we want to hear from you.

Please contact us at:
maintleyapp@gmail.com

When reaching out, please include:

The page or feature you are having trouble with

A brief description of the issue

The device and browser you are using (if possible)

We will make reasonable efforts to address concerns promptly.
