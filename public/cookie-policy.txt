1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that allow the Service to:

Recognize your device during and between visits

Remember your preferences and settings

Improve functionality, performance, and user experience

Collect anonymous analytics for Service improvement

Cookies may also include similar technologies such as local storage, web beacons, or pixels.

2. Types of Cookies We May Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the basic operation of Maintley (e.g., login sessions, account access)

Cannot be disabled without limiting functionality

Performance / Analytics Cookies

Collect anonymous information about usage, crashes, or feature engagement

Help us understand how the Service is used and improve it over time

Functional Cookies (Optional / Future Use)

Remember user preferences (language, theme, settings)

Maintley does not use cookies for advertising, tracking third-party activity, or selling personal information.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

Provide and maintain the Service efficiently

Monitor performance and detect errors or crashes

Understand feature usage for product improvement

Remember user preferences for convenience

All analytics and tracking data is anonymous and does not identify individual users.
